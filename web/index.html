Historical snapshot of Gitcoin Grants Stack era data, preserved for reproducibility and research.

Gitcoin sunset Grants Labs and Grants Stack on May 31, 2025. Hosted apps are no longer maintained. For current and broader Gitcoin data, use Open Source Observer. See the governance post and OSO datasets docs.
