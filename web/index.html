Grants Data Portal

Open source, serverless, and local-first Data Platform for Grants Data. Access the data you need to conduct research and drive the community forward!

Main Ideas

This portal gathers and processes grant data to offer key insights and enable data-driven decision making.

It harnesses modern data pipelines and storage techniques to ensure transparent and reproducible results.
